Who Were the Signers?

Most Famous Signers

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence. Later became the 3rd President of the United States and is remembered as a champion of democratic ideals.
John Adams
A key advocate for independence in Congress. He later served as the 2nd President of the United States and was a central Founding Father.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned statesman, scientist, inventor, and diplomat. He symbolized American ingenuity and wisdom, with a reputation that extended worldwide.
John Hancock
President of the Second Continental Congress and famous for his bold, oversized signature on the Declaration. His name became synonymous with “signature.”
Samuel Adams
A leading organizer of resistance against British rule, including the Boston Tea Party. He later became governor of Massachusetts and was known as a fiery revolutionary.
