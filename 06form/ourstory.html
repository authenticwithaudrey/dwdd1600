Our Story

We are a family-run food truck based in Great Falls, Montana. The business began in 2016 with a simple ground stand and pop-up tents, building slowly over the years until we were able to purchase and build our truck. The food truck exists because a mother wanted to create a real way to teach her children the value of hard work and to build something together as a family. The foods on our menu are novelty items we have experienced throughout our lives and loved so much that we wanted to bring them to other people. Before any equipment or walls existed, the idea lived on a small paper drawing. That drawing became the blueprint for everything that followed, and it now hangs framed beside our ordering window as a reminder of how far the dream has come.
